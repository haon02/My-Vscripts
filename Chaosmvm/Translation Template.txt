Replace the "english" with whatever language you are translating for
ignore any color information, such as "\x01", "/x07######", or "/x08########"
also ignore modifying the subsitute strings, such as "%s" and "%%", or the "\n" (newline) characters
dotn change any strings that specify commands, such as "/itemhelp #" (i might get to making this work)

an example one would look like this

japanese = {
    LOW_METAL = "メタルが足りない" (sorry for if this is butchered, just used google translate)
